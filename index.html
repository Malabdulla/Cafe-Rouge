Welcome to Cafe Rouge

Located in the vibrant dining district of Adliya (Villa 42, Road 2601), Cafe Rouge offers a sophisticated retreat from the bustling city. Our passion is to serve culinary creations that delight both the eye and the palate.

From our traditional Bahraini and English breakfasts to our sizzling steaks, artisan pastas, and crafted mocktails, every dish is prepared with fresh, premium ingredients by our master chefs.
